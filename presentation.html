Healthcare Delivered to Your Doorstep

Project Overview

📌 Problem Statement
Healthcare accessibility remains a major challenge. Long hospital wait times, travel inconvenience, and high costs create barriers to quality medical care, especially for elderly and busy professionals.
🎯 Objective
Deliver convenient, affordable, and professional healthcare directly to patients' homes, eliminating travel hassles and reducing wait times with verified medical professionals available 24/7.
📝 Project Summary
A responsive web platform connecting certified doctors and healthcare professionals for home-based medical services including consultations, nursing care, physiotherapy, lab tests, elderly care, and emergency visits with transparent pricing and insurance support.
